Our little garden

A gentle first step into the big, wonderful world.

Founded in 2002, Little Flower Balvihar has spent more than two decades helping our youngest learners feel safe, loved and curious. We believe small children learn best when they are happy — so our days are full of stories, songs, play and plenty of warm hugs.

Every child grows at their own pace. Our caring, trained teachers keep batches small, celebrate little wins, and turn everyday moments into joyful learning — from holding a crayon for the first time to making their very first friend.

And while the children play, parents can relax: our campus is fully CCTV-monitored, clean, and built around their safety.

Our journey

Growing together since 2002

2002 — It all began

Little Flower Balvihar opened its doors in Latur with one simple wish: a safe, joyful place for little ones to begin.

Growing family

Word spread, classrooms filled, and generations of happy children blossomed with us.

Two branches

We grew to two campuses across Latur so more families could join our little garden.

Safer & smarter

CCTV monitoring, a 1:10 ratio and play-based Kreedo learning brought modern safety and discovery to our classrooms.

20+ years of smiles

Two decades on, we’re still doing what we love — helping little flowers bloom.
